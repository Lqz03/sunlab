i-STEM

Scaffoldia MyMaths

Led by Dr. Sun Daner, our research team at The Education University of Hong Kong explores the transformative potential of emerging technologies in education. We focus on AI-powered learning systems, technology-enhanced STEM education, and innovative pedagogical approaches that foster higher-order thinking across disciplines. Our work bridges educational research and real-world implementation—from developing ScaffoldiaMyMaths (https://www.scaffoldia.com/zh-hans/) with adaptive AI scaffolding for students with mathematics learning difficulties, to creating iSTEM (http://isteminquiry.com/), a design thinking-driven platform for aerospace-focused STEM education. Through rigorous research and meaningful collaboration with schools and communities, we strive to create equitable, evidence-based educational solutions that prepare learners for an AI-driven future. Join us in reimagining education for the 21st century.
